    The Guy She Was Interested in Wasn’t a Guy at All

    sweet and comedic story about a tomboy and a popular girl. updates are fast but extremely short which can be frustrating.

    My Sister’s Best Friend, My Lover

    very angsty but recent chapters have been surprisingly uplifting. i want this girl to be happy so badly

    Luminous=Blue

    lots of drama and emotional tension with a resolution so sweet it makes me kick my feet up in the air and roll around laughing bc of how happy it makes me whenever i reread it after going a few months without it

    Handsome Girl and Sheltered Girl

    funny, heartwarming and relatively story by mochi au lait who also draws some much more ~problematic~ stuff

    Trying Out Marriage With My Female Friend

    cute and just recently hit its emotional climax though its not actually over yet, i’m predicting theres going to be one more chapter of wrapping up

    I Decided to Fake a Marriage With My Junior(♀️) to Shut My Parents Up

    really short but has “person who’s never understood what love is falls in love” which is one of my absolute favorite tropes.

    An Easy Introduction to Love Triangles (To Pass the Exam!)

    the very first page starts with “who decided that you could only love one person” which is something i’ve always been asking. the feelings of the main characters in this one resonated with me so much
